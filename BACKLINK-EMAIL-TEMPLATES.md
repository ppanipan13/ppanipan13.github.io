## ✅ Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips:
- ✅ Keep it under 50 characters
- ✅ Be specific (mention article name if applicable)
- ✅ Avoid spam words (FREE, URGENT, CLICK NOW)
- ✅ Example: "Backlink Request - Portfolio for Featured Article"

### Email Body Tips:
- ✅ Personalize (use their name, mention specific article)
- ✅ Keep it short (3-5 paragraphs max)
- ✅ Make ask clear and easy
- ✅ Provide value in return
- ✅ Include clickable links
- ✅ Professional signature with links

### Follow-up Strategy:
- ✅ First email: Week 1
- ✅ First follow-up: After 5-7 days
- ✅ Second follow-up: After 10-14 days
- ✅ Move on after 3 attempts

## 📊 Tracking Backlinks

### Tools to Monitor:
- **Google Search Console** - See linking domains
- **Ahrefs** - See all backlinks (paid)
- **SEMrush** - Backlink audit (paid)
- **Moz** - Domain authority tracking (free tier available)
- **Majestic** - Backlink analysis (free tier)

### Check Monthly:
- How many new backlinks?
- From which domains?
- What anchor text?
- Domain authority of linking site?

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Quality over Quantity**
   - 1 backlink from authoritative source > 10 backlinks from low-quality sites
   - TechBullion link > spam blog link

2. **Relevance Matters**
   - Maritime publication > random blog
   - Professional org > social media

3. **Anchor Text Optimization**
   - Use: "Panipan Pansang" or "maritime founder"
   - Avoid: "click here" or "website"

4. **Natural Link Building**
   - Make genuine relationships
   - Offer value first
   - Ask for links naturally
